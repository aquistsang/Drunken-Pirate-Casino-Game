🍺 Drunken Archer – How to Play

97% RTP · 3% house edge

  • You are the archer! Place your bet ($0.01–$100).
  • Shoot Fire an arrow from your hand toward the apple.
  • Hit the apple Multiplier doubles! 1× → 2× → 4× → 8× …
  • Miss the apple The arrow may fly past or hit the person — either way you lose your bet.
  • Cash out Keep winnings (bet × multiplier) anytime — even while the arrow is in flight!
  • Fair 97% RTP A slight 3% house edge keeps the tavern running!
  • Season Each round fills the Season bar. When full, the tavern gets a new name!
